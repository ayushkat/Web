A little bit about my roots

I was born on an island called Bermuda and I lived there for about half of my life. It was honestly great, being able to wake up every morning by a beach and getting the chance watch sunsets by the sea shore was truly amazing. The beauty of the island is inexplicable so if you're not worried about the conspiracy theories on the Bermuda triangle then you should definetely visit the place.
